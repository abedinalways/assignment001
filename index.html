Pillars Of "Banglasdesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation focuses on fostering creativity and technological advancements, driving progress in sectors like agriculture, healthcare, and education. Through initiatives such as "Digital Bangladesh," the country emphasizes digital transformation, encourages startups, and invests heavily in the IT and communication sectors to enhance global competitiveness

Sustainability

Sustainability plays a crucial role in addressing environmental challenges and promoting eco-friendly policies. With a strong emphasis on sustainable agriculture, renewable energy, and green industries, Bangladesh is taking proactive steps to combat climate change

Empowerment

Empowerment aims to uplift citizens by focusing on education, skill development, and entrepreneurship, particularly for women and youth. By promoting gender equality, social inclusion, and poverty reduction, Bangladesh works to create a more equitable society. Grassroots participation in decision-making processes further strengthens this pillar

Infrastructure

Infrastructure underpins the nation’s progress by investing in transportation networks, energy grids, and digital systems. Mega-projects like the Padma Bridge and Dhaka Metro Rail significantly enhance connectivity and commerce, while rural and urban infrastructure improvements support economic activities and improve quality of life
